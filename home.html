NFL Teams

An NFL team is a professional football franchise that competes in the National Football League (NFL), the top level of American football. There are 32 teams, split into two conferences: the AFC (American Football Conference) and the NFC (National Football Conference). Each team has a unique history, culture, fanbase, and playing style.Teams compete throughout the season for a chance to make the playoffs, with the ultimate goal of winning the Super Bowl, the biggest championship in American sports. Whether you prefer a historic powerhouse, an underdog, or a team with a strong local identity, there’s an NFL team that’s perfect for you.

NFL Rules

Each team plays 17 regular-season games, competing for a spot in the playoffs, which lead to the ultimate championship game the Super Bowl. The league is known for its fast-paced action, strategic gameplay, and passionate fan culture.Games are played weekly from September to February, with teams battling for division titles, playoff berths, and a shot at football glory.
